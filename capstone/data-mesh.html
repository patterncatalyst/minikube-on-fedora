Capstone · Data mesh

A data mesh on minikube.

The capstone puts every building block from §1–§12 together into one coherent system: a domain of services modeling order-placement-through-shipment, each owning its data, its API surface, and its operational lifecycle, communicating through a deliberate mix of REST, gRPC, GraphQL, and Kafka.

Where this fits in the tutorial

This capstone is §17 of the main tutorial. The earlier sections introduced the building blocks individually — helm, Istio, KEDA, and the rest. The capstone is where they converge into a single running system, and the section that justifies the time invested in §1–§12.

One command brings the whole thing up; the host prerequisites (including the rootless-podman and kernel tweaks) are covered in §1.
